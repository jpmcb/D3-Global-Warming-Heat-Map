But is the planet really warming up?

Lets see what the facts have to say

This is a graph representing global average surface temperatures given in Celcius from the last 300 years.

"The earth has warmed since 1880. Most of the warming occurred in the past 35 years, with 15 of the 16 warmest years on record occurring since 2001.
The year 2015 was the first time the global average temperatures were 1 degree Celsius or more above the 1880-1899 average.
Even though the 2000s witnessed a solar output decline resulting in an unusually deep solar minimum in 2007-2009,
surface temperatures continue to increase."
NASA
